MySafety GDPR Privacy Policy

MySafety is part of the University of Birmingham Dubai team project for team67dub. This privacy policy will explain how our web Application uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

MySafety is dedicated to safeguarding users' personal/sensitive information and privacy. In compliance with GDPR laws, continuous reviews and improving policies/procedures are conducted to ensure a high level of data privacy and protection.

What data do we collect?

MySafety gathers personal information essential for providing its safety services. This includes:

  • Personal information which user provides during registration, such as name, phone number, email address, age, height, and emergency contacts.
  • Location data: Realtime GPS coordinates when the user activates the “SOS” button.
  • User generated content, including voice recordings and chat messages during emergencies and any additional information which has been voluntarily provided during emergency situations.
  • No special category or financial data will be stored on my Safety.

Processing of Data for:

MySafety processes personal data to:

  • Enable live location sharing through the SOS button for immediate assistance.
  • Identifying danger zones based on SOS activations for informed decision-making.
  • Facilitate communication through voice recordings and chat tools.

How the data is collected:

Data is collected through user interactions with the app, including registration, SOS activation, location services activation and usage of communication features.

How will we use your data:

MySafety collects data to enhance your personal safety which includes real-time communication, personalized assistance during emergencies, and danger zone identification.

Where do we Store your data:

The servers hosting the data for the MySafety app are located within the EEA (European economic Area), specifically in the United Kingdom. This strategic choice of server location ensures compliance with data protection regulations, especially the GDPR. The UK, being part of the EEA, adheres strongly to the GDPR, which sets high standards for the protection of personal data. Storing user data within the UK region ensures strong compliance with the legal framework and principles outlined in the GDPR. By choosing the UK/EEA, MySafety is committed to prioritizing the privacy and security of user data. We aim to provide users with the assurance that their personal information is stored and processed in compliance with the highest data protection standards.

Why we collect your data:

  • Enable live location sharing for swift assistance during emergencies.
  • Identify any danger zones based on SOS activations for informed decision making.
  • Facilitate communication through voice recordings and chat tools.
  • Tailor responses and services to individual needs based on user provided information.

Legal justification for Data Processing:

User consent serves as the legal justification for processing personal data. Users explicitly provide consent during the registration process and emergency activations.

Data Deletion and retention:

MySafety retains user data only as long as it is necessary to deliver its services and comply with the legal requirements. Once that data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized following GDPR regulations. This will be till after exams, as that is what has been highlighted by the Module Lead. After exams, all data will be deleted.

Sharing of Personal Information:

MySafety is committed to providing you with the best and most secure safety services. To improve our service delivery, we may need to share personal information, including your location with trusted service providers. We want to be transparent about this process, ensuring that your data is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable regulations, which includes the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Trusted service providers: MySafety may collaborate with external service providers to consistently offer you improved and optimized services.

Exercising your Rights

To exercise any of the rights mentioned, you may contact MySafety through the app's designated support channels or via email. We will respond to your request within 7-14 days.

Security Measures

MySafety implements data encryption, access limits, and regular backups to ensure the security of users’ personal information and the confidentiality/integrity of user data.

Verification Process

To ensure the security and privacy of your data, we may need to request additional information to verify your identity before processing your request. This is to prevent unauthorized access to your personal information.

Marketing

MySafety does not use personal information for marketing purposes.

What are cookies?

Small text files which are placed on your device when you use our app. They help us enhance user experience by remembering your preferences and providing content that suits your needs and therefore analyzing the app performance.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: These are necessary for the basic functionality of our app, e.g., cookies that keep you logged in.
  • Authentication cookies: These cookies are crucial for user authentication after log in, ensuring that you remain logged in as you navigate through the different sections of the app.
  • Identification and authentication cookies: We use cookies to uniquely identify your device and authorize your access to specific features and resources within the MySafety app.
  • Password Hashing cookies: For enhanced security, we use cookies to securely hash and store your password, ensuring that sensitive information is protected.
  • Analytics cookies: These help us understand how you interact with the app and enable us to improve features/overall performance. The information is anonymized and aggregated.
  • Functionality cookies: These enhance your experience by remembering your preferences, e.g., frequency use of SOS button.

How we use cookies:

We use cookies to make your interactions with the app more efficient and personalized. They help us analyze app usage patterns, identify areas for improvement, and enhance overall performance. It also stores your preferences making it easier for you to navigate and access relevant content.

Consent Mechanism:

By using the MySafety app, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. We provide a cookie banner during the app's onboarding process, which will allow you to manage your cookie preferences.

Managing your Cookie preferences:

You have the option to control and manage your cookie preferences. This can be done through the app settings, where you can choose to accept or reject certain types of cookies.

Third-party cookies:

MySafety does not use third-party cookies for tracking and advertising purposes. All cookies used within the MySafety app are for essential functions related to user authentication, identification, authorization, and password hashing.

Duration cookies:

Session cookies, which are deleted when you close the app, and persistent cookies, which are stored for a specific duration, enhancing your experience over multiple sessions.
